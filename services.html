Our Services

Modern Physiotherapy with Hands-On Care

Every treatment plan at Hands-On Physio is personalized to your condition, movement limitations, goals, and stage of recovery. We combine manual therapy, movement rehabilitation, and exercise prescription to help you recover efficiently and safely.

Manual Therapy

Hands-on physiotherapy techniques focused on reducing pain, improving mobility, restoring joint movement, and improving muscle function.

Exercise Rehabilitation

Guided rehabilitation exercises designed to restore strength, flexibility, balance, and movement confidence.

Sports Injury Physiotherapy

Rehabilitation programs for athletic injuries, overuse conditions, mobility restrictions, and return-to-sport recovery.

ICBC Physiotherapy

Treatment and rehabilitation support for motor vehicle accident injuries with direct billing options available.

WorkSafeBC Rehabilitation

Recovery-focused physiotherapy for workplace injuries with individualized treatment planning and functional rehabilitation.

Post-Surgical Rehabilitation

Structured rehabilitation after orthopedic procedures to restore mobility, strength, and movement function.
