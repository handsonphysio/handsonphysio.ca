About Hands-On Physio

Hands-On Physio is built on a simple principle: every patient deserves undivided attention. We provide one-on-one physiotherapy care, ensuring your physiotherapist is fully focused on you throughout your entire session.

Our approach combines hands-on treatment when clinically appropriate, detailed movement assessment, and individualized rehabilitation programs designed around your goals, lifestyle, and recovery needs.

We do not double book patients. Every appointment is dedicated to one person only.
